Hammond PSD Financial Audit Report

Audit Period: 2019–2025

Prepared by: Independent Financial Review

Executive Summary

This audit reveals a disturbing lack of financial discipline, transparency, and ethical accountability within Hammond PSD. The organization consistently fails to meet basic standards for public fund stewardship, displays a persistent disregard for proper accounting principles, and has used ratepayer dollars to enrich its administrative structure while deferring necessary infrastructure investment.

Key Audit Findings

  • No General Ledger: Hammond PSD does not maintain a proper accounting ledger.
  • Administrative Payroll Escalation: Payroll has increased over $65,000 with no service improvements.
  • Missing Grant Reporting: Over $1M in grants are not reflected in budgets or audits.
  • Rate Increases Masking Operational Losses: Projected deficits despite higher billing.
  • Insider Transactions: Asset sales and bonuses benefiting board-connected individuals.

Financial Red Flags Summary

Issue Severity Status
No general ledger / unreliable books Critical Ongoing
Administrative payroll abuse High Escalating
Grant funding missing from reports High Unaccounted
Bonus programs without oversight Moderate–High Entrenched
Surplus asset sales lacking bids High Ongoing
Water loss >30% for 5+ years High Unsolved
Audit warnings ignored repeatedly Critical Unresolved

Recommendations

  1. Immediate forensic audit by WV State Auditor’s Office
  2. PSC re-review of all rate increases submitted since 2019
  3. Freeze on all bonuses and incentive programs until publicly justified
  4. Mandatory disclosure of administrative compensation
  5. Investigation by WV Ethics Commission into insider transactions
  6. Suspension of surplus sales until public bid protocols are adopted
  7. Replacement of administrative leadership if compliance is not achieved within 6 months
